Welcome to StarzGazer

StarzGazer is an app built by stargazers - for stargazers.

The cosmos is as mysterious and beautiful as it is impossibly vast, and we want to share our love of that spl with people like you. Pick a star, a planet, a system, or even just a color - and we'll show you the infinite wonders of the night sky.

Join our community and point your gaze skyward with us - it's fast, easy, and free.
